uOttawa Bionics

Co-Founder and Technical Captain

August 2017 - June 2019

I co-founded uOttawa Bionics back in 2017 with the goal of designing an exoskeleton that could be used for stroke rehabilitation.
After experiencing a stroke, patients are often left with musclular atrophy and lack of coordination, often cumulating in re-learning how to walk properly.
Our goal was simple; build a tool that could assist the user throughout their recovery, providing both muscular support, and gait correction. The cumulation of that work is AllonStride. Click here for a video!

Contribution to the Team

While on the team, I:

  • Designed, simulated and manufactured various parts, including the "arms" and motor mounts
  • Wrote a PID controller to manipulate the motors
  • Managed the team budget and sponsorship, and ensured milestones were met
